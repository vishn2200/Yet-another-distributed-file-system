to make a directory:
python3 client.py mkdir [dir_name]

to upload a file:
python3 client.py put [file_path] root/[destination]

to get/downlaod a file:
python3 client.py get [directory_name]

to move a directory:
python3 client.py move [source] [destination]

to copy a directory:
python3 client.py copy [source] [destination]

to delete a directory:
python3 client.py delete [path_to _directory]
